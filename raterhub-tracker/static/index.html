Quick start guide

Get up and running in minutes with simple steps for new and returning users.

  • A
    Sign up. Click "Start free trial" and create your account with email and password. Confirm your email to secure your workspace.
  • B
    Log in. Use your credentials to access the web app or sign in with your configured SSO provider.
  • C
    Install the Chrome extension. Visit the Chrome Web Store listing, click "Add to Chrome," and pin it to your toolbar for quick access. Sign in once to sync with your account.
  • D
    Start tracking. Launch your first session from the extension or the dashboard and watch your metrics populate instantly.

FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about getting started and daily use.

Can I track multiple projects?

Yes. Create projects or tags for each client or queue, and switch between them from the extension or dashboard.

Do I need to keep a tab open for tracking?

No. The Chrome extension runs quietly while you work and syncs entries to the dashboard automatically.

What if I forget to stop a session?

Use the timeline editor to trim sessions or add manual entries so your reports stay accurate.

How do exports work?

Generate CSVs or PDFs from the reporting page with a date range, then share them with your lead or upload to your HR tools.

Is my data secure?

We encrypt data at rest and in transit, and apply least-privilege access controls for your workspace.
